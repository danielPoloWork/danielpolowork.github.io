Authorization to process personal data

The authorization to process personal data has become, in recent years, a fundamental issue for those involved in personnel selection but also for those looking for work.

The new European GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) on Privacy, of 2018 May 25, is a very decisive aspect that can affect the taking charge of the job application. The inclusion of the authorization to process personal data has become an essential clause for it to be taken into consideration by recruiters.

The inclusion of the authorization to the processing of personal data authorizes, be it the employer and / or the human resources manager, to the processing of personal data. Thanks to this authorization, the employer, or whoever for it, can use the data present to proceed with the selection, or can keep them pending future job searches.

What is personal data?

Personal data means all information that identifies a person and provides details about his characteristics, habits, personal relationships, etc. They are divided into three different categories:

  • Identification data: any information that allows the direct identification of natural or legal persons, bodies or associations (e.g. name, surname, address, email, telephone number..);
  • Sensitive data: those relating to the ethnicity of origin, political opinions, religious beliefs, state of health..;
  • Legal data: those that indicate the possible presence of judicial measures (e.g. criminal convictions, alternative measures to detention..).

What is meant by the processing of personal data?

The processing of personal data therefore means any operation carried out, paper or with the support of electronic tools, which concerns the collection, consultation, use, storage, processing, modification, dissemination and cancellation of the data.

What changed with the new GDPR privacy?

With the entry into force in Italy, from 2018 September 19, of Legislative Decree 101/2018, with the aim of adapting Italian legislation to the European GDPR, it was established that consent to the processing of personal data is not mandatory. However, each individual can freely choose to insert it or not and that is why I decided to use it.
